About Educated Nepal

Our Mission

At Educated Nepal, our mission is to empower Nepali students with high-quality educational resources, fostering a culture of learning and academic excellence. We strive to make education accessible, engaging, and effective for students in grades 10 to 12.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every Nepali student has equal access to world-class educational resources, enabling them to realize their full potential and contribute to the nation's progress.

Our Story

Educated Nepal was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate educators and tech enthusiasts who recognized the need for a comprehensive online learning platform tailored to the Nepali education system. What started as a small project has now grown into a trusted resource for thousands of students across Nepal.

What We Offer

  • Comprehensive study materials for grades 10, 11, and 12
  • Interactive quizzes and practice questions
  • Downloadable notes and textbooks
  • Past exam papers and model question sets
  • Mobile app for offline access to resources
  • Community forums for peer-to-peer learning
